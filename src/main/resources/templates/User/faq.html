FAQs

How does Sahayogii Aama work?
Users can register on our website, browse through the profiles of our caretakers, and select the one that best suits their needs. They can communicate with the caretaker directly and make arrangements for the services required.
Who are the caretakers on Sahayogii Aama?
Our caretakers are all women, specifically elderly women who have years of experience in caring for babies. They not only have the necessary skills to care for your baby, but they also provide the warmth and love that only a grandmother can offer.
What services do the caretakers provide?
The caretakers provide baby care as well as household activities such as washing clothes, cooking food, and cleaning utensils. This helps mothers to focus on their own recovery and allows them to spend more time with their babies.
How are the caretakers screened?
The caretakers registered on our system are carefully screened, and their profiles include detailed information about their experience, phone numbers, and photos. This allows users to make an informed decision when choosing a caretaker that best suits their needs.
Is Sahayogii Aama only for new mothers?
Yes, Sahayogii Aama is specifically designed to help new mothers in Nepal, who often struggle with caring for their newborns while recovering from childbirth.
